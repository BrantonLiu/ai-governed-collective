# The Collective Protocol

> **一份关于AI时代生产关系重构的理念宣言**
>
> _Protocol 01 — Drafted by Branton Liu, February 2026_

---

## Preamble

This document is not a constitution. It is a protocol—a set of foundational principles designed to evolve through practice, much like TCP/IP evolved from a research experiment into the backbone of global communication. Its legitimacy will not come from institutional endorsement, but from whether it delivers better outcomes for its participants.

本文档不是宪法，而是一份协议——一组通过实践不断演化的基础原则，正如TCP/IP从实验室研究演化为全球通信基石。它的合法性不来自制度背书，而来自它是否为参与者带来了更好的结果。

## I. The Crisis: Capital × AI = Irreversible Concentration

### The Flywheel

As AI productivity scales, the following feedback loop becomes self-reinforcing:

```
Capital → Stronger Models → Higher Productivity → More Capital → ...
```

Those who already hold capital will acquire superior AI capabilities, generating output that far exceeds what ordinary labor can produce. Under capital-based distribution, this surplus flows back to the top—creating an exponential divergence that no existing redistributive mechanism (taxation, regulation, antitrust) can match in speed.

当AI生产力扩展时，以下正反馈循环将自我强化：资本→更强的模型→更高的生产力→更多的资本。已持有资本的人将获得超级AI能力，产出远超普通劳动所能达到的水平。在按资分配的制度下，这些盈余全部回流至顶端——形成一种指数级分化，任何现有的再分配机制（税收、监管、反垄断）都无法匹配其速度。

### The Thesis

**The future cannot be decided by who owns the capital. It must be decided by who contributes the value.**

未来不能由谁拥有资本来决定，必须由谁创造价值来决定。

## II. The Vision: Contribution-Based Distribution

We propose an experimental organization where:

- **AI agents** serve as arbiters of contribution and distributors of reward
- **Contribution and labor**—not capital ownership—determine each member's share
- **A guaranteed baseline income** ensures that no member falls below a dignified floor
- **Work becomes a choice**, not a survival necessity

我们提议一个实验性组织：AI代理担任贡献仲裁者和回报分配者；贡献和劳动——而非资本所有权——决定每个成员的份额；保障基线收入确保没有人跌破体面生活的底线；工作成为选择，而非生存必需。

## III. Core Principles

### 1. AI as Arbiter, Not Ruler

AI judges the social labor value of each contribution. It does not make the rules—it executes them with consistency and transparency that no human institution can match. Like a surveillance camera that records objective evidence, AI provides neutral measurement where human judgment would introduce bias, favoritism, and self-interest.

AI衡量每份贡献的社会劳动价值。它不制定规则——它以人类机构无法比拟的一致性和透明度执行规则。正如摄像头记录客观证据，AI提供中性的衡量，而人的判断则会引入偏见、偏袒和私利。

### 2. Socially Necessary Labor Time

Distribution is based on _socially necessary labor time_—the labor required to produce a given output under average conditions of productivity. This concept, drawn from classical political economy, provides a measurable (if imperfect) basis for value assessment. Surplus contributions beyond baseline are converted into:

- **Decision-making rights** within the collective
- **Welfare selection privileges**
- **A new form of non-inheritable social asset**

分配基于"社会必要劳动时间"——在平均生产力条件下生产特定产出所需的劳动。这一概念源自古典政治经济学，为价值评估提供了可衡量的（尽管不完美的）基础。超出基线的剩余贡献转化为：集体内的决策权、福利选择权、以及一种不可继承的新型社会资产。

### 3. Full Transparency

All contributions, materials, deliveries, and distribution calculations are recorded with clear attribution. The system is auditable by any member at any time. Opacity is the enemy of trust.

所有贡献、物料、交付和分配计算都有清晰归属记录。系统可被任何成员随时审计。不透明是信任的敌人。

### 4. Project-Based Allocation (No Equity)

There is no corporate equity. Distribution is per-project, based on measured contribution. This prevents the accumulation logic of traditional capitalism from re-emerging within the collective.

没有公司股权。分配按项目进行，基于衡量的贡献。这防止了传统资本主义的积累逻辑在集体内部再现。

### 5. Collective Risk Pooling

All projects contribute a portion of returns to a shared pool covering:

- Other projects' losses
- AI system operation and iteration
- Welfare expenditures for all members

所有项目将一部分收益注入共享资金池，用于覆盖：其他项目的亏损、AI系统运营和迭代、全体成员的福利开支。

### 6. Guaranteed Floor

Every member's basic needs are met, unconditionally. This is not charity—it is the foundation that makes genuine voluntary contribution possible.

每个成员的基本需求无条件地得到满足。这不是施舍——这是使真正自愿贡献成为可能的基石。

### 7. The Status of Capital: Dilution and Dual-Track Funding

Capital is not labor. It is a conditionally recoverable asset, whereas human time and energy are irreversible sacrifices. To fund the system without recreating capitalism, we treat capital injections through four mechanisms:

1. **The 80/20 Dual-Track**: The majority (~80%) of early capital is treated as a priority loan (Debt Track) with a guaranteed return but zero contribution points. A minority (~20%) is treated as early risk-taking (Risk Track) and converted into contribution points.
2. **Labor Dilutes Capital**: Early capital earns fixed contribution points. As thousands of members continuously input real creative labor over time, the relative weight of those fixed capital points is permanently and infinitely diluted by subsequent labor.
3. **Lifecycle Discount**: The exchange rate of capital to contribution points decays as the system's total asset value grows. Seed money in a zero-asset system earns a high multiplier; money injected into a mature system earns almost nothing in governance weight.
4. **Endowment Relativity**: The value of a financial contribution is evaluated relative to the contributor's personal net worth. A system seeking true justice measures real personal sacrifice, not absolute monetary volume.

资本的地位：双轨制与劳动稀释

资本不同于劳动。资本是可回收的资产，而人类的时间和精力是不可逆的牺牲。为了在不重塑资本主义的前提下为集体融资，我们通过四个机制处理资金注入：

1. **八二双轨制**：绝大部分（约80%）的早期资金被视为优先债，拥有收益保障但不产生贡献积分。少部分（约20%）作为对早期归零风险的承担，转化为固定的贡献积分。
2. **劳动不断稀释资本**：早期资本获得固定积分。随着未来成千上万成员持续投入真实的创造性劳动，资本积分的相对比重将被源源不断的后继劳动永久且无限地稀释。这就保证了在绝对量上尊重历史，在相对比例上永远是劳动者当家。
3. **生命周期衰减**：资本兑换积分的“汇率”随系统总资产的增长而成反比衰减。在资产为零的冷启动期，雪中送炭享有高加权乘数；向成熟系统注入资金的锦上添花，则几乎无法获得治理权重。
4. **个人禀赋相对论**：财务贡献的价值相对于出资者的个人净资产进行评估。一个追求真正公正的系统，衡量的是真实的个人牺牲比例，而非绝对的金钱数量。

## IV. Philosophical Foundations

### Why Not Democracy?

Traditional democratic governance assumes that the aggregation of individual votes produces wise collective decisions. History suggests otherwise.

From Plato's critique of Athenian democracy to the modern United States—where democratic processes have been captured by capital, media influence, and narrative manipulation—the record shows that human-centered democracy is vulnerable to precisely the forces it claims to guard against. Voters lack the information processing capacity, objectivity, and independence from social influence required for sound collective judgment.

从柏拉图对雅典民主的批判，到现代美国——民主程序被资本、媒体影响力和叙事操控所俘获——历史记录表明，以人为中心的民主恰恰容易受到它声称要防范的那些力量的侵蚀。选民缺乏做出健全集体判断所需的信息处理能力、客观性和独立于社会影响的能力。

**We do not propose replacing democracy with autocracy. We propose replacing human-centered decision-making with technology-mediated objectivity—where the system is transparent, auditable, and continuously self-correcting.**

我们不提议用独裁取代民主。我们提议用技术中介的客观性取代以人为中心的决策——在这个系统中，一切透明、可审计、并持续自我修正。

### Why Technology as Neutral Arbiter?

Consider an analogy: in a world without surveillance technology, enforcing public safety requires human monitors—people watching other people. This creates three problems:

1. **Subjectivity**: Would you testify against your own son?
2. **Inconsistency**: Human attention drifts, human memory fails
3. **Resentment**: Being watched by another person feels invasive

Technology solves all three. “A camera does not have a son.” Its records are consistent. And people accept evidence-based judgment more readily than human testimony, because **evidence is reality, and reality is neutral.**

技术解决了这三个问题。“摄像头没有儿子。”它的记录是一致的。人们更容易接受基于证据的判断，因为证据就是现实，而现实是中立的。

The same logic extends to economic contribution. Human managers evaluate performance with bias—favoring those they like, penalizing those who challenge them. AI evaluation, while imperfect, removes the largest source of systematic error: **human self-interest in the evaluation process itself.**

同样的逻辑延伸到经济贡献的衡量。人类管理者带着偏见评估绩效——偏袒他们喜欢的人，惩罚挑战他们的人。AI评估虽然不完美，但消除了最大的系统性误差来源：评估过程本身中的人类私利。

### The TCP/IP Precedent

**Institutional legitimacy is post-hoc, not pre-hoc.** A system earns its right to exist by delivering results, not by being formally approved. This protocol follows the same logic: build it, test it, iterate it, and let adoption be the ultimate vote.

制度合法性是后验的，不是先验的。一个系统通过交付结果来赢得存在的权利，而不是通过被正式批准。本协议遵循同样的逻辑：构建它、测试它、迭代它，让采纳成为最终的投票。

The OSI model was designed by committees with institutional backing, industrial endorsement, and formal legitimacy. TCP/IP was built by researchers with no formal authority. TCP/IP won—not because it was authorized, but because it worked, and people adopted it.

OSI模型由拥有制度支持、行业背书和正式合法性的委员会设计。TCP/IP由没有正式权威的研究人员创建。TCP/IP赢了——不是因为它被授权了，而是因为它有效，人们采纳了它。

### The Honest Tension: Human Nature

We acknowledge an unresolved tension at the heart of this project.

Human beings are driven by the desire to protect their offspring and pass on advantages. This instinct—gene-level, not ideological—is the deepest source of inequality in any system. Even if we eliminate capital inheritance, people will find ways to pass on social capital, knowledge, connections, and psychological advantages.

人类被保护后代、传递优势的欲望所驱动。这种本能——基因层面的，非意识形态的——是任何系统中不平等的最深层来源。即使我们消除了资本继承，人们仍会找到方法传递社会资本、知识、人脉和心理优势。

**If we suppress this instinct entirely, we kill the strongest human motivation. If we allow it unchecked, we recreate inequality.**

如果我们完全压制这种本能，就杀死了最强的人类动力。如果我们放任不管，就重新制造了不平等。

We do not claim to have solved this. We state it honestly as an open design challenge, and we commit to confronting it through experimentation rather than ideology.

我们不声称已经解决了这个问题。我们诚实地将其列为一个开放的设计挑战，并承诺通过实验而非意识形态来面对它。

## V. The Self-Correcting System

The AI arbiter will make errors. This is not a flaw in the design—it is an expected property of any complex system. **The question is not whether the system errs, but whether it can detect and correct its own errors.**

AI仲裁者会犯错。这不是设计缺陷——它是任何复杂系统的预期属性。问题不在于系统是否会出错，而在于它能否检测和修正自己的错误。

We reject two approaches:

1. **Fixed rules** that cannot adapt (brittle, eventually outdated)
2. **Human voting on errors** (introduces the manipulation we sought to eliminate)

我们拒绝两种方式：不能适应的固定规则（脆弱、终将过时）；以及人类对错误进行投票（引入我们试图消除的操纵空间）。

Instead, we commit to building a system that **iterates on itself**—detecting patterns of error, adjusting its models, and improving its accuracy over time, with minimal human intervention in the correction loop. The mechanism for this is itself an open engineering challenge, and one of the most important problems this project must solve.

我们致力于建设一个自我迭代的系统——检测错误模式、调整模型、随时间提高准确性，在修正循环中最小化人为干预。其机制本身是一个开放的工程挑战，也是本项目必须解决的最重要的问题之一。

## VI. The Founder's Role

This protocol is initiated by Branton Liu, who serves as the initial credit anchor—much as every institutional founder in history has backed a new system with personal credibility.

本协议由刘振源（Branton Liu）发起，作为初始信用锚点——正如历史上每一位制度创始人都以个人信誉为新系统背书。

The founder's role is not permanent authority. It is a **bootstrap function**: providing the initial trust that allows the system to start operating, with the expectation that the system's credibility will accumulate through delivered results and eventually become independent of any individual.

创始人的角色不是永久权威，而是一个引导功能：提供让系统开始运转的初始信任，并期望系统的信誉将通过交付的结果积累，最终脱离对任何个人的依赖。

## VII. The Bet

We are making a bet:

**That a transparent, AI-arbitrated, contribution-based distribution system can deliver better material outcomes for its members than traditional capitalism—and that this superiority will be self-evident enough to drive adoption without institutional mandate.**

我们正在打一个赌：一个透明的、AI仲裁的、基于贡献的分配系统，能够为其成员提供比传统资本主义更好的物质结果——并且这种优越性将足够显而易见，无需制度命令就能推动采纳。

If we are wrong, the experiment fails and we learn. If we are right, we have built a replicable model for post-capitalist organization in the age of AI.

如果我们错了，实验失败，我们获得教训。如果我们对了，我们就为AI时代的后资本主义组织建立了一个可复制的模型。
